## 🚀 How to Play

1. **Select a Champion** from the character selection screen
2. **Enter Battle** against the Automaton Grizzly
3. **Choose Your Strategy:**
   - Use Basic Attacks to build up energy
   - Deploy Elemental Attacks for moderate damage
   - Save energy for devastating Ultimate attacks
4. **Watch Your Resources:**
   - Monitor health bars for both fighters
   - Manage energy for special abilities
   - Pay attention to cooldown timers
5. **Victory Conditions:**
   - Reduce enemy health to 0 to win
   - Survive enemy attacks and counterattack

## 🎯 Combat Tips

- **Energy Management:** Don't waste energy on weak enemies
- **Timing:** Use Ultimate attacks when enemy health is high
- **Critical Hits:** Elemental and Ultimate attacks have higher crit chances
- **Armor:** Heavy armor reduces all incoming damage
- **Cooldowns:** Plan your attack sequence around cooldown timers
